Cyclone Impact in Botswana

Overview

A recent cyclone affected Botswana, causing heavy rainfall, strong winds, and disruptions to daily life.

Impact

Homes were damaged, roads were flooded, and communities faced major challenges due to the cyclone's effects.

Response

The government and aid organizations provided relief efforts, including temporary shelters and food assistance.
